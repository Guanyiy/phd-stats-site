I‚Äôm **Guanyi**, an applied statistician whose work focuses on the methodological challenges that arise when cognitive data are collected across diverse educational, linguistic, and cultural contexts. **üåè Cross-National Modeling (HRS-HCAP, Mex-Cog, LASI-DAD):**

My methodological interests align closely with the research philosophy in my graduate statements and current projects:
